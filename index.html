Every HVAC installation in Colorado Springs requires a permit from the Pikes Peak Regional Building Department (PPRBD). This is not optional — it is Colorado law. Unpermitted HVAC work can void manufacturer warranties, create code violations that surface during home inspections, and expose homeowners to liability.

Colorado Springs Elite HVAC handles the entire permit process: we submit the application, schedule the inspection, and ensure every installation meets current mechanical code requirements. Our technicians are trained on PPRBD-specific requirements including high-altitude gas appliance derating, combustion air calculations, and venting specifications for Colorado Springs' 6,035-foot elevation.

When you hire Colorado Springs Elite HVAC, you receive a code-compliant installation with proper documentation — no shortcuts, no risk to your warranty or your home's resale value.
